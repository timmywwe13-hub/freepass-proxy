==============================================
  HOW TO PUT FREEPASS ONLINE (Free Hosting)
==============================================

You will use two free websites:
  • GitHub  — stores your code
  • Render  — runs your server online

No credit card needed for either one.

----------------------------------------------
STEP 1 — Make a free GitHub account
----------------------------------------------
1. Go to https://github.com
2. Click "Sign up" — use any email
3. Verify your email

----------------------------------------------
STEP 2 — Upload your files to GitHub
----------------------------------------------
1. Go to https://github.com/new
2. Repository name: freepass-proxy
3. Set it to "Public"
4. Click "Create repository"
5. On the next page click "uploading an existing file"
6. Drag and drop these 3 files:
     - server.js
     - index.html
     - package.json
7. Click "Commit changes"

----------------------------------------------
STEP 3 — Deploy on Render (free hosting)
----------------------------------------------
1. Go to https://render.com
2. Click "Get Started" → sign up with your GitHub account
3. Click "New +" → "Web Service"
4. Connect your GitHub account if asked
5. Find "freepass-proxy" in the list → click Connect
6. Fill in these settings:
     Name:         freepass-proxy  (or anything you want)
     Runtime:      Node
     Build Command: (leave blank)
     Start Command: node server.js
     Plan:          Free
7. Click "Create Web Service"
8. Wait 2-3 minutes while it builds

----------------------------------------------
STEP 4 — Get your URL
----------------------------------------------
After it finishes building, Render gives you a URL like:
   https://freepass-proxy.onrender.com

That's your personal proxy! Share it with anyone.
Open it on your Chromebook, phone, anything — it works!

----------------------------------------------
NOTES
----------------------------------------------
• Free Render servers "sleep" after 15 min of no use
  — first visit after sleeping takes ~30 seconds to wake up
  — after that it's fast

• To keep it always awake (optional):
  Go to your service on Render → Settings → 
  add a "Health Check" URL of /

• Your URL is public — anyone who has it can use it
